About Modifix

Automation and production support built on real food & beverage experience.

Who we are

Modifix is a South Australian technical services business supporting the entire food & beverage supply chain. We work with producers, processors, packaging lines, storage facilities and dispatch operations to keep equipment running safely, efficiently and reliably.

With hands-on experience across fillers, labellers, conveyors, pigging, line control and safety systems, Modifix focuses on practical solutions that work in live production environments – not just on paper.

How we work

  • Listen first: understand your process, symptoms and constraints.
  • Identify true causes: fix the real issue, not just the loudest alarm.
  • Offer options: quick fixes vs. longer-term improvements with clear trade-offs.
  • Deliver cleanly: neat work, tidy documentation and minimal disruption.
